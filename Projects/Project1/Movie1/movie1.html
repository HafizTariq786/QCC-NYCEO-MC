# Idiots' Synopsis

3 Idiots is a 2009 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani who co-wrote the screenplay with Abhijat Joshi, based on Chetan Bhagat's novel Five Point Someone. Starring Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, Boman Irani and Omi Vaidya, the film follows the friendship of three students at an Indian engineering college and is a satire about the social pressures under an Indian education system.[7][8][9] The film is narrated through parallel dramas, one in the present and the other ten years in the past.
